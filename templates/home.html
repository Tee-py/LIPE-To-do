LIPE-TODO

Keep track of your daily Todos and longterm Projects with LIPE-TODO.

Daily Todos

Create a list of daily tasks you wish to perform and start working on the tasks. Also allows you to delete and update/edit an existing task and also mark tasks you have completed.

Projects

Create longterm projects with due date and also break projects to smaller tasks with completion time and start working. Also allows you to delete and update subtasks when neccessary and mark subtasks as done
